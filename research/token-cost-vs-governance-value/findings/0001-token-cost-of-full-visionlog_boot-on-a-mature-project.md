## Claim

A mature project (10 SOPs ~497 tokens each, 10 guardrails ~200 tokens each, vision ~300, goals ~500) costs ~7,770 tokens per full boot read. Over 20 sessions (100 tasks), that is 155,400 tokens in governance overhead alone — before any work is done.
