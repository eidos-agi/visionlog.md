## Claim

Reading full SOP bodies before every task costs ~4,970 tokens × 100 tasks = 497,000 tokens on a mature project. This is the worst pattern — it makes the governance overhead dwarf the actual work in almost every realistic scenario.

## Supporting Evidence

> **Evidence: [MODERATE]** — Measured from real SOP files, extrapolated to 10 SOPs at current average body size (2026-03-22), retrieved 2026-03-22
